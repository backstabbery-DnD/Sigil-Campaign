    Session 2

    As we arrive in Session 2 we are in the same spot we were at the end of Session 1 the lower ward, a slum area full of quirky characters. We encounter a small, ramshackle carnival that has a high paced game of five finger fillet, we pass on taking a turn. We continue walking and come upon a nicer tent. There is a sexy red head in front of the tent who tries enticing all of us to spend some time with her. As she propositions each of us she shifts into what she thinks would be most enticing to us. For Corvis she turns into a Catrina. She's a changeling hooker. Lonkee gives her spellscar jerky, she eats the jerky and starts tripping balls. Says she sees something that isn't there.

    We walk on and a little further on but still in the market area there are [[gnoll]] performers (clowns) and a small boy picking up coins; possibly soul coins. The gnolls are gnawing on bones, Corvis determines they are juvenille human or elven child bones. Its a group of 8 gnolls. We decide to attack as Corvis and myself are offended at the idea of eating slaves, especially child slaves. Reece walks into their tent that they are living in like he owns the place and I follow him, hoping to draw the gnolls in. They follow us.

    The fight begins and Reece targets a group of 4 on our left. Corvis frontlines to the leader. I get to use the Dragonwing Shortbow for the first time and put out around 50+ damage in one turn. Lonkee attacks the group on the right. By our second round all the gnolls are dead or dying. We keep the leader alive and bring him over to a fire to question him. Who runs the slave trade? seems decentralized Who is your local contact? the fated, they have the biggest holdings and their tendrils are everywhere Who is your specific contact? Marcella with the fated. There is more debate where the gnoll tries to imply he bought the slaves fair and sqaure so he can eat them if he wants to. This actually seems true to some extent although it seems strictly forbidden to buy slaves as food ( you can murder / eat them if they misbehave, but you can't just eat them because you are hungry ). Corvis kicks him into the fire to die, then heals him to make him suffer more. We briefly loot and Corvis takes the bag of coins from the boy, sorts out the soul coins and puts them in a puch on his belt. 11 soul coins in total.

    We are immediately confronted by a group of Mercykillers who say that we are being charged with Improper Assembly, Murder, Illegal Fires, Improper Disposal of Bodies, Disturbing the Public, etc... They are going to arrest us and take us to the court district. Corvis talks his way into letting us keep our weapons and no cuffs if we go willingly. We ask to be presented to Guvner Neverbloom.

    I ask if the Mercykillers were looking for us specifically, but they weren't they just happened to be in the area.

    We end up in prison row. Reece casts prestidigitation to clean us off and the Mercykillers don't like that and say we violated our agreement to go peacefully. They try to cuff us, but as a group we are all pretty much against that and willing to fight for it. We realize a fight is not ideal as we are in an area with multiple Mercykillers patrols. We are also right next to where Guvner Neverbloom should be working. There are guvner guards and we yell to them that we need to speak to Guvner Neverbloom. The Mercykillers try to stop us but we tell the guards they Mercykillers are unfit, did the crimes and are trying to pin it on us. The guards says "are these the ones getting kickback from Marcella?". The mercy killers arent happy about this but the guards are going to let us see Guvner Neverbloom. To add a little "fuck you" flavor I hand the head Mercykillers guard a business card and tell him to call me if he ever needs my help and walk past him to the Guvners court area.

    One of the guveners guard proposes a debate vs the Mercykillers, the Mercykillers accepts and they start debating. The guvner guard eventually wins the debate and the mercy killer vanishes. Corvis casts zone of truth and asks one of the remaining Mercykillers if he took kickbacks from the slavers. he responds "i didn't".

    The debate is over, the Mercykillers are walking away. We are on the steps of the courthouse. The Mercykillers prison is just across the square we are in. We are about to head inside and Lonkee eats a piece of spellscar jerky. She looks up and transports to the top of the court house where there is a Christmas feast waiting for her. She starts devoring the ham and throws some of the sides down for other people to eat. The guvner guards call for someone to bring her down from the top of the court house. An elysian knight ridiing a pegasus shows up to get Lonkee off the roof. The knight says to the guards something like "Don't ever call us for this kind of stuff again." and then flies away.

    We find out the kids name is Petra, he's a boy, he was enslaved by the knolls and we rescued him, he's sticking with us and Farmer Pait is looking after him.

    we are brought into a courtroom where there are 10 guards and 3 guvners, 1 of which is Guvner Neverbloom. They seem to be a group of 3 lawful judges - lawful good in the center, lawful evil to the right and lawful neutral to the left. Guvner Neverbloom is a lawful evil hag. The guards address the court and talk about the Mercykillers trying to arrest us but then losing a debate with the guards. Guvner Neverbloom smiles at this. Corvis bows to her and says "we were sent by a friend from the astral". When Corvis says that she stops smiling at us. She asks what does Mysterious Mage Female need. We explain about the Aoskar blink. Guvner Neverbloom says we should go to a bar and wait for her, but then asks why we were being escorted by mercykillers. We explain we were trying to save children from slavery / being eaten - but they seem ok with it and agree that we did something wrong. In the courtroom it seems you can't lie, you take dmg if you lie. There is some talk of favors and the Aoskar blink and also about corrupt Mercykillers taking bribes from slavers. We offer to help Guvner Neverbloom with the Mercykillers problem in exchange for sending Farmer Pait and Petra home. There's some more talk about how many favors this is. Guvner Neverbloom finally agrees but is reluctant to deputize us but i say i've been deputized so many times it would just be one more in a long list. They wait to see if I take damage but when I don't they agree to our plan.

    The lawful neutral judge is a Catrina, in fact the same Catrina that the changeling hooker turned into. We have a short discussion with her and she offers us a Mimir to help us on our investigation.

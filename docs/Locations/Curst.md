### Core Concept and Alignment

- **Classification:** A Gate-Town located in the Ringlands of the Outlands.

- **Connected Plane:** It holds the permanent portal to **Carceri** (specifically linking to its first layer, Orthrys).

- **Alignment:** Neutral Evil / Chaotic Evil.

- **Core Function:** It serves as a grim border town, prison, and dumping ground for exiles, refugees, and criminals from across the multiverse.


### Geography and Layout

- **The Structure:** The city is perfectly circular, surrounded by a massive wall.

- **The Districts:** Inside the walls, the town is laid out in five concentric ringed districts (or "ring roads") that wrap around the central plaza.

- The outermost ring contains common housing, stables, and taverns.

- The inner rings contain workshops, upscale estates, and government administration.
  
- **The Gate:** At the absolute center of the city sits **Gate Square**. The portal to Carceri stands here, appearing as a large, four-sided archway constructed entirely out of razorvine that radiates a deep red glow.
   

### Planar Mechanics and Environmental Hazards

- **Razorvine:** The city walls, buildings, and structures are heavily choked and overgrown with dangerous razorvine, a plant capable of slicing through thick leather armor.

- **The Weight Effect ("Ball and Chain"):** Due to its proximity to Carceri, all creatures within the town suffer from a supernatural physical drag. Movement speeds for all individuals are mechanically reduced by 5 feet.
   
- **Dimensional Anchor:** Teleportation magic and plane shifting do not function within Curst. Portals exiting the town are nearly non-existent; the only reliable planar exit is the gate directly into Carceri.

- **The Threat of Sliding:** Because the population’s shared mindset heavily mirrors the betrayal and despair of Carceri, Curst is at constant risk of a "planar slide"—a phenomenon where the entire city could permanently shift out of the Outlands and merge into Carceri itself.
   

### Key Landmarks and Factions

- **The Curst Prison:** A massive, underground labyrinth of spikes, bars, and towers that runs directly beneath the entire settlement. It is guarded exclusively by the Wall Watch.

- **The Wall Watch:** The permanent military force of the city. They man the gates, track all incoming names, and restrict citizens from leaving. They do not officially rule, but they maintain absolute authority over the city's perimeter.

- **The Burghermaster's Estate:** The official mansion of the town's ruler. In current canon, the estate and the current administration are heavily subverted by _Demodands_ (Gehreleths), the native fiends of Carceri.

- **The Town Dump:** A massive junkyard spanning the valleys outside the city walls. Items randomly lost across the multiverse frequently materialize here. It is occupied by rust monsters, cranium rats, and a green hag named Dolores.

- **The Traitor's Gate Tavern:** A notorious local establishment run by an unfriendly planar fighter named Tainted Barse.

- **Active Underground Factions:** The town contains highly active hidden cells of both the classic _Anarchists_ (Revolutionary League) and the _Hands of Havoc_. It also serves as a operational hub for _Hezobol_, a prominent wizard who coordinates slave trafficking between Curst and the Gehreleths of Carceri.

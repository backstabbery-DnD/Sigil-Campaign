### 1. The Clerk’s Ward (The Bureaucratic Heart)

This is the domain of scholars, lawyers, scribes, and middle-class planar merchants. It is clean, heavily patrolled, and remarkably quiet compared to the rest of the city.

- **The Vibe:** Elegant stone buildings, sprawling libraries, and quiet cafes. The bureaucracy of the multiverse is filed away here.

- **Key Landmarks:** The **Hall of Speakers** (the city’s parliament where laws are debated) and the **Civic Festhall** (headquarters of the Society of Sensation, offering incredible sensory magic and performances).

- **Party Note:** The _Harmonium_ watch is dense here. An open brawl will get you tossed into a cell within minutes. Everything must look civilized.


### 2. The Lower Ward (The Industrial Forge)

The industrial powerhouse of the city, named "Lower" because it is choked with smoke and foul sulfur rising from portals that link directly to the Lower Planes (like the Nine Hells and the Abyss).

- **The Vibe:** Clanging iron, massive ironworks, choking smog, and constant heat. It is a gritty, blue-collar district powered by magic and sweat.

- **Key Landmarks:** The **Great Foundry** (run by the _Believers of the Source_, who forge the city's weapons and infrastructure) and the **Armory** (where the city’s defensive siege weapons are kept locked up tight).

- **Party Note:** The visibility is terrible due to the smog, making it a fantastic place for setting up an ambush. Just watch out for industrial accidents or high-level elemental hazards.


### 3. The Lady’s Ward (The High-Class Citadel)

The wealthiest, safest, and most arrogant neighborhood in Sigil. It is home to the city’s aristocrats, rich planar elites, and the most powerful faction headquarters.

- **The Vibe:** Towering palaces, opulence, marble pillars, and beautifully maintained plazas.

- **Key Landmarks:** The **City Court** (run by the _Guvners_), the **Barracks** (headquarters of the _Harmonium_), and the ominous **Prison** (run by the [[Mercykillers]]).

- **Party Note:** You need a good reason and some official paperwork to wander this ward looking rugged. The guards here will stop and question anyone who looks like a mercenary.


### 4. The Market Ward (The Trade Hub)

If it exists anywhere in the infinite Outer Planes, you can buy it, sell it, or trade it in the Market Ward. It is a loud, sprawling, multi-cultural marketplace that never sleeps.

- **The Vibe:** A chaotic sensory overload of exotic creatures, strange languages, bizarre magical smells, and endless rows of merchant tents and permanent bazaars.

- **Key Landmarks:** The **Great Bazaar** (a chaotic open-air market covering several square miles) and the **Grand Bazaar Vaults** where the most dangerous artifacts are guarded.

- **Party Note:** You can melt into a crowd of hundreds of different planar races instantly. You need high perception to track anything here.


### 5. The Guildhall Ward (The Working Class District)

Sandwiched right next to the Market Ward, this is where the people who actually keep the markets supplied live and work. It is populated by cross-planar craftsmen, tradesmen, and apprentices.

- **The Vibe:** Row houses, guild houses, bustling workshops, and neighborhood taverns. It is crowded but generally orderly, as the guilds enforce their own peace.

- **Key Landmarks:** The various ancestral Guildhouses (The Blacksmiths, the Alchemists, the Portal-Mappers).

- **Party Note:** The locals here protect their own. If you try to drag a guild member out of his workshop you might find an entire neighborhood of angry workers blocking your exit.


### 6. The Hive (The Slums & Underbelly)

The largest, most populated, and most terrifying ward in Sigil. The Hive is a massive, chaotic slum where the law completely ceases to exist.

- **The Vibe:** Total squalor, crumbling tenement shacks, lawlessness, and desperate violence. It is home to the destitute, the diseased, tiefling gangs, and members of the _Bleak Cabal_ or _Dustmen_.

- **Key Landmarks:** The **Mortuary** (the massive fortress where the _Dustmen_ process all the corpses of the city) and the **Gatehouse** (the asylum run by the _Bleakers_).

- **Party Note:** This is an incredibly dangerous area. The Harmonium never patrols here. In the Hive, you can use whatever lethal force you want but if you get into trouble, absolutely no one is coming to save you.

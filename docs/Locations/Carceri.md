
### Plane Properties and Alignment

- **Official Names:** Carceri, The Tarterian Depths, or Tartarus.
   
- **Planar Alignment:** Neutral Evil / Chaotic Evil.
   
- **Cosmic Classification:** Outer Plane.
   
- **Core Concept:** A planar prison. It is designed to prevent escape; portals function as one-way entrances, and leaving requires specific, rare keys or divine intervention.
   

### Structure and Layers

Carceri consists of six distinct layers. Each layer is a vast space containing an infinite number of self-contained, spherical planets (orbs) that drift near each other.

|**Layer**|**Canonical Name**|**Environmental Characteristics**|
|---|---|---|
|**1st Layer**|**Orthrys**|A realm of vast swamps, quicksand, stagnant rivers, and volcanic mountains.|
|**2nd Layer**|**Cathrys**|A layer covered in dense jungles and grasslands where the plant life produces corrosive, flesh-eating acid.|
|**3rd Layer**|**Minethys**|A desert environment perpetually subject to abrasive sandstorms that erode stone and organic material.|
|**4th Layer**|**Colothys**|A landscape composed entirely of immense, vertical stone canyons, chasms, and mountain spires.|
|**5th Layer**|**Porphatys**|A shallow, sub-zero ocean of black water subjected to acidic snowfall.|
|**6th Layer**|**Agathys**|A single, infinite sphere of solid black ice streaked with frozen red matter. It is the coldest layer.|

### Inhabitants and Factions

- **The Demodands (Gehreleths):** The native fiends of Carceri. They are divided into three distinct castes (Farastu, Kelubar, and Shator) and serve as the wardens of the plane, tasked with preventing prisoners from escaping.

- **The Titans:** A pantheon of ancient, giant-statured deities imprisoned in the bogs of Orthrys after losing a war against the Olympian gods.

- **The Bastion of Last Hope:** A hidden fortress located on the layer of Orthrys, which serves as a major base of operations for the Revolutionary League (Anarchists) faction.

- **The Tower of Khin-Oin:** A 24-mile-high fortress shaped like a spinal column, located on the layer of Othrys, controlled by the Yugoloths.

- **Petitioners:** The souls of the deceased who were untrustworthy, treacherous, or backstabbers in life. They retain their memories but suffer from extreme paranoia, preventing them from cooperating with one another to organize an escape.

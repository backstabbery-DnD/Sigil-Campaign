
Core Concept
---
Rangers are elite wilderness survivalists, trackers, and scouts who bridge the gap between civilization and the untamed wilds. Equal parts warrior and nature-weaver, they use a blend of martial prowess, stealth, and primal magic to hunt down specific threats—whether those threats are marauding monsters, ancient dragons, or planar intruders.

Key Mechanics & Features
---
Favored Enemy & Deft Explorer: Rangers are specialized hunters. They possess unparalleled knowledge of specific creature types and terrain, granting them bonuses to tracking, recalling lore, climbing, swimming, and navigating otherwise treacherous environments.

Primal Spellcasting: Drawing power from the natural world (similar to druids), rangers use magic to augment their combat and utility. Signature spells include Hunter's Mark (to track and deal extra damage to a target) and Pass Without a Trace (to shroud the party in near-perfect stealth).

Fighting Style: At 2nd level, rangers adopt a specialized combat style. While famous for Archery, they are equally deadly in close quarters utilizing Two-Weapon Fighting or defensive martial arts.

Ranger Archetypes (Popular Subclasses)
---
A ranger's archetype reflects how they adapt to and master their chosen hunting grounds:

Hunter: The classic sentinel of the wilds. They focus on pure martial techniques designed to take down specific threats, specializing in either slaying massive giants or thin out overwhelming hordes of smaller foes.

Beast Master: A warrior who shares a deep, magical bond with a primal beast companion. The ranger and their beast (such as a hawk, wolf, or bear) act as a synchronized unit, moving and attacking together on the battlefield.

Gloom Stalker: Masters of the dark, often found in the Underdark or deep caverns. They are terrifyingly lethal during the first round of combat, possess darkvision that functions even against creatures with darkvision, and are entirely invisible while in pitch darkness.

Horizon Walker: Planar guardians who protect the material realm from extraplanar threats. They can sense nearby planar portals, magically teleport short distances between attacks, and convert their weapon damage into pure force energy.

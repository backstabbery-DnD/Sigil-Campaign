### The Triad of Law (The City Government)

1.  **The Fraternity of Order (Guvners)**
	- **Philosophy:** They believe the multiverse is governed by absolute laws. If you learn the legal loopholes of reality, you can rewrite existence.   
	- **Headquarters:** The High Courts (The City Court)
2. **The Harmonium (Hardheads)**  
	- **Philosophy:** Peace can only be achieved through absolute, forced uniformity. If everyone agrees to obey the law, conflict ceases to exist.
	- **Headquarters:** The City Barracks
3. **The Mercykillers (Jailers / The Red Death)**
	- **Philosophy:** Mercy is a crime against justice. Every crime must face its exact, cold retribution without exception.
	- **Headquarters:** The Prison

###  The Cosmic & Personal Growth Seekers

4. **The Athar (Defiers)**
	- **Philosophy:** Gods are frauds. They are just highly powerful extraplanar entities, not divine beings deserving of mortal worship.
	- **Headquarters:** The Shattered Temple
5. **The Mind's Eye (Seekers)**
	- _Note: This is a new faction formed by the merger of the classic Godsmen and Signers._
	- **Philosophy:** The multiverse is a forge meant to refine individuals. By testing yourself and shaping reality, any mortal can transcend to godhood.
	- **Headquarters:** The Great Foundry
6. **The Society of Sensation (Sensates)**
	- **Philosophy:** The multiverse only exists if you experience it. Truth is found solely through sensory input, art, danger, and luxury.
	- **Headquarters:** The Civic Festhall
7. **The Transcendent Order (Ciphers)**
	- **Philosophy:** Hesitation invites failure. True harmony with the multiverse means acting entirely on pure, unclouded instinct without overthinking.
	- **Headquarters:** The Great Gymnasium

###  The Materialists & Individualists

8. **The Fated (Takers)**
	- **Philosophy:** Might makes right. If you have the power to take something and hold onto it, it belongs to you by natural right.
	- **Headquarters:** The Hall of Records (The Tax Collectors)

###  The Nihilists & Destructive Powers

9. **The Bleak Cabal (Bleakers)**
	- **Philosophy:** The multiverse makes absolutely no sense, and life has zero inherent meaning. You must find your own small purpose by helping others cope with the madness.
	- **Headquarters:** The Gatehouse (The City Asylum)
10. **The Heralds of Dust (Dusters)**
	- _Note: Previously known simply as the Dustmen._
	- **Philosophy:** This life is a cruel illusion. We are all already dead, and the only goal is to achieve "True Death"—purging all emotional attachments to find absolute peace.
	- **Headquarters:** The Mortuary
11. **The Doomguard (Sinkers)**
	- **Philosophy:** Entropy is the only absolute truth. Everything eventually decays, breaks down, and dies—and it is a beautiful thing to witness.
	- **Headquarters:** The Armory

### 5. The Chaos Vector

12. **The Hands of Havoc (Wreakers)**
	- _Note: A new ascendant blend of the old Xaositects and the Revolutionary League._
	- **Philosophy:** Tyranny must be burned away. Real freedom only exists when oppressive structures, organizations, and laws are systematically dismantled by chaos.
	- **Headquarters:** Scattered warehouses throughout the slums of the Hive

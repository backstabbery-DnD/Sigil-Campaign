### The Visual Design

- **The Core Icon:** The badge prominently displays a stylized, crimson red **winged sword** or a **winged dagger**.

- **The Imagery:** The blade points downward, symbolizing the swift execution of justice, while the outstretched wings surrounding the hilt represent the faction's reach across the planes to hunt down lawbreakers.

- **Colors:** The primary colors utilized on the emblem are deep blood red, black, and cold iron gray or silver.


### Materials and Wear

- **Materials:** High-ranking members and Justiciars wear badges forged from dark, heavy metals such as iron, cold iron, or blackened steel.

- **Placement:** Members typically wear the emblem pinned openly to their armor, heavy leather coats, or uniform cloaks to clearly establish their authority to bystanders and law enforcement units like the Harmonium.


### Functional Purpose

- **Identification:** The badge serves as official identification within Sigil and the Outlands, granting the bearer entry into faction-controlled areas like the Prison in the Lady's Ward.

- **Symbol of Authority:** It visually signifies to citizens and criminals that the wearer possesses the authority to pursue fugitives, conduct investigations, and carry out sentences mandated by the City Courts.
